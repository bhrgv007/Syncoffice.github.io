Products

SYNC CLOUD

“Sync Cloud” A cloud document management system to manage and store your files in a centralized location and helps business to reduce dependence upon space-consuming. With “Sync Cloud” files are often easily shared together with your colleagues and clients within the office, also as those performing work from home from external locations.




SYNC CLOCK

“Sync Clock” lets you decide the priorities of your daily schedules in the way you want. Its automatic real-time tracking features makes it comprehensive centralized cloud system that stops wasting productivity time.




SYNC ACC-RECO

Sync Office Account Reconciliations software changes the process of reconciliation and manage every aspect of business. Our modernized account reconciliation software ensures accurate and efficient accounting activities, and eliminate manual spreadsheet-dependent processes prone to human error.




SYNC PASSWORD

Generate and store powerful passwords in encrypted cloud with “Sync Password manager”.With "Sync Password," end-users can quickly reset their own passwords and access accounts.




SYNC LEAVE

Automate the leave workflow to eliminate tedious work and extra cost. Sync Leave automates the employee's leave data from leave allotting, grants, cancelling, to tracking the furlough.
